# Gated Reverb Distortion
Mk.gee's electric guitar tone has gained popularity in recent years and I seek to emulate it. Many would say that Mk.gee's music generally falls under the "dream pop" genre, mixed with dark, liminal atmosphere. An integral part of his tone is the Rainger FX Reverb X pedal, a gated reverb and distortion pedal that serves to create gigantic swells and ringing.
